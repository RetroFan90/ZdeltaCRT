
## Quickstart

1. **Whitepaper**: `docs/whitepaper.md` → PDF via Pandoc or LaTeX  
2. **BOM**: `bom/axis_bom.csv` import into your parts manager  
3. **HDL**: `scripts/fpga/zdelta_core.v` + `zdelta_tb.v` in Vivado/ModelSim  
4. **Schematics**: Use the `.md` guides in `schematics/` to drive your CAD  
5. **Render**: Hand `render/exploded_view_layout.txt` to your concept artist
